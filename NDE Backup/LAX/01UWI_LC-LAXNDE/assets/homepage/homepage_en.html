Search@UW

Search@UW provides simple, one-stop searching for articles, books, videos, digital media, government documents, and more.

You should use Search@UW when you want to:

  • Find books and articles using one search
  • Do a broad, multidisciplinary search
  • Use a variety of information sources
  • Find books or media that UW-La Crosse owns
  • Request books or media that another library owns
