What is Search@UW?

Search@UW is a one-stop search tool for articles, books, videos, digital media, government documents, and more.

Search@UW is especially good for:

  • Finding books and/or articles in a single search
  • Doing a broad, multidisciplinary search
  • Accessing a variety of information sources

The default search includes all UW System campus libraries’ holdings, allowing you to make borrowing requests as needed. You can also opt to search articles to which UWM does not have instant access so that you can make Interlibrary Loan requests quickly and easily.
